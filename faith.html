Harvest Statement of Faith

we believe in...

The Bible is the inspired and infalliable Word of God.

2 Timothy 3:15-16

One God, eternally existent in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.

Deuteronomy 6:4, Matthew 18:19, Mark 12:29

The virgin birth of Christ, His vicarious and atoning death, bodily resurrection and Ascension.

Isaiah 7:44, Romans 8:34, Acts 1:10

Salvation through the blood of Jesus Christ.

1 John 5:10, Romans 10:13-15

Water baptism by immersion.

Matthew 8:19

The Lord's Supper - wine and bread, dellowship and memorial of His death.

1 Corinthians 11:26

The baptism of the Holy Spirit.

Acts 2:4, Acts 10:44-46, Acts 15:8-9

Divine healing through the redemptive work of Christ on the cross.

Isaiah 53:4; Matthew 8:16, 17; James 5:13, 14

The sanctification power of the Holy Spirit which enables a believer to live a holy life.

Hebrews 12:14; 1 Peter 1:15, 16

The second coming of Jesus Christ.

Acts 24:15; Luke 14:14; Revelation 19:7, 9

The blessed hope, resurrection and translation of those who have fallen asleep in Christ and those who are alive at His coming.

1 Thessalonians 4:16, 17

The final judgement of those who have not accepted Christ as Savior along with the devil and his angels in the Lake of Fire.

Revelation 19:20, 20:11-15, 21:8
